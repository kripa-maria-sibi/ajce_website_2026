About AJCE

Amal Jyothi College of Engineering is a premier institution dedicated to providing world-class engineering education. Located in Kottayam, Kerala, AJCE has been a beacon of technical excellence for over three decades.

Our mission is to cultivate technologically competent engineers who are socially responsible and environmentally conscious. With a focus on innovation, research, and industry partnerships, we prepare students to tackle global challenges.

Admissions

Undergraduate Programs: AJCE offers 4-year BTech programs in multiple specializations. Admissions are based on merit and entrance examinations.

Postgraduate Programs: Advanced MTech programs available for specialized fields including research-oriented studies.

Entry Requirements:

  • For BTech: 10+2 with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics
  • Merit-based and entrance exam-based admissions
  • Application deadline: June-July for regular admissions
  • Scholarships and financial aid available for deserving candidates

Contact the Admissions Office for detailed information about programs, fees, and application procedures.
